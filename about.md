## Writing about stuff that makes my life in IT easier and more interesting.

My name is Ørjan Steindal. I am a Field IT Manager in Microsoft located in Oslo (Norway). Although I have strong focus on MS technology, being that it is what I use a lot of the time, my goal is not to promote or sell anything. The blog is my own and a hobby.

Technology is constantly changing (NEWS FLASH!) and as an IT generalist I get exposed to new (to me) things almost daily. Often I do share instructions and tips via email, chat etc. More often I pick up new ways of solving things. Blogging can be a good way of documenting this. My level of expertice varies depending on the topic, hopefully the sum of my experiences will add up to providing something useful for someone. So, basically it is about (constantly) learning IT.
